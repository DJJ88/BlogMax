XEmacs Bug Fix

Tuesday, August 21, 2001

I fixed some bugs that made BlogMax fail to work in XEmacs 21.4 on Windoze (and maybe elsewhere). line-end-position wasn't defined there and file-relative-name had a bad habit of returning backslash characters instead of forward slash. I curse Bill Gates regularly about that.
